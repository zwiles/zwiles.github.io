Final Project Idea

My final project idea is to track what ads Facebook shows me based on my recent searches. I learned that I can learn about what Facebook knows about me like political party, age, and race. After analyzing that data, I want to see if based on what I search and sites I vist, Facebook displays certain ads. I want to create three or four different experiments using selenium searches. In the searches, they will visit a website I commonly use, log in to my account if I have one, and look around, maybe start to make a purchase but just live it in the cart. Based on that, I would like to see if Facebook will show me more ads based on what recently did on google. The websites I will use is an airline I commonly use, a store I shop online, just looking through makeup site and maybe looking at reviews, and maybe a restaurant place.
It might also be interesting to create a fake Facebook account and see if I can see if I can manipulate what Facebook knows about this person. I can do this by liking pages and posts on Facebook. Then by creating certain searches on Google, if Facebook shows certain ads. Facebook has already collected data on me so it would be interesting to see what they do with a new user because Facebook makes money on its ads.
To show my results, I will write a script to collect every ad that shows on Facebook. I will then see which ads or for the sites I had recently used.
